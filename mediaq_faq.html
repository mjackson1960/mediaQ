    We recommend that you wear white so that the color will show better, but you are welcome to wear whatever you choose. Remember that the color may not wash out of the clothing so don't wear any thing that you wouldn't want to get stained.

    White isn't absolutely necessary, but your colors will show better if you wear white.

    You will see smiling faces along the course whose main goal is to douse you in colorful powder to ensure that you look like you ran through a colorful rainbow by the time you cross the finish line.

    The color does mostly wash out after the event. As with anything dirty, the sooner you wash it the better. We suggest wearing items that you wouldn't mind getting colorful. If you would like to preserve the color in your running shirt, spray it with vinegar and iron it. If you wash your shirt...the color will eventually come out.

    The color powder used for this event is non-toxic and free of heavy metals. The bright colors are cornstartch and FD&C dyes. As with any substance, you will want to keep it out of yor eyes and are color throwers will aim low as you pass by. Participants may wish to wear glasses or goggles for their eyes and use a bandana or dust mask for there mouths.

    We will have some blow driers available at the finish line. With a good dusting off, you won't be 100% clean but you should be good for the drive home.

    We recommend that you bring some towels for the inside of your car, are a change of clothes just in case.
